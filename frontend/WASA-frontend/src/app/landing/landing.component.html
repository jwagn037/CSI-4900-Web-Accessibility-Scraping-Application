WASA

Welcome to WASA

The WASA (Web Accessibility Scraping Application) project is an ongoing CSI 4900 honours project at the University of Ottawa for the development of an application intended to parse inaccessible content for users with disabilities, serving up the scraped content in an accessible way for each user's individual needs.

Submit the URL of an article you want to read, and we do the rest.
